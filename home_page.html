About

NCT 127 is the second sub-unit of NCT. They made their debut as a seven member boy group on July 7, 2016 with the release of "Firetruck". The number 127 refers to the longitude for Seoul, where they are based. The group made a come back on January 6, 2017 with two new members, Johnny and Doyoung. On September 17, 2018, Jungwoo was announced as the unit's newest member ahead of their comeback. The group sings in Korean, Japanese, and English; and has become a K-Pop phenomenon, regularly topping the Korean and Japanese musical charts. Internationally, they have peaked at the top 3 chart in Billboard 200 with their album "Sticker -- The 3rd Album" (2021), and stayed on it for 17 weeks.
